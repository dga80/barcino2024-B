About Us
Our Story,Vision & Values

Pooling over 50 years of industry experience, Barcino Meats was created in early 2022 by three industry experts.

Our directors have incomparable knowledge in sourcing, buying and selling the highest quality butchery products from Spain to the UK.

By having dedicated and diligent employees in both the UK and Spain, we are able to provide a tailored and unique experience for all of our customers.

Our customer base ranges from retail businesses, to manufacturers, as well as wholesalers all over the UK and Ireland.

The diversity of our customer base means we cater for all sizes of firms. Whether you need one pallet, or one-hundred trucks, we can sort it! We also take care of all the import documentation for our customers’ orders, meaning there will be no unnecessary delays or setbacks through Customs.

At Barcino Meats, we understand how fast paced and volatile the meat manufacturing industry can be, which is why we use innovative and exciting technologies to provide our customers with premium products in the most safe and efficient ways.

We take pride in the quality of both our fresh and frozen produce, achieving great-tasting results in our BRC accredited factory every single day.

Our dedicated inhouse technical team work closely with our trusted suppliers to ensure that food safety and hygiene are up to the Barcino Meats Ltd standard.

Our Products

Barcino Meats is committed to providing top-quality meat products, ensuring high welfare standards for the animals. Our pork selection, including renowned products like Jamón Ibérico and Spanish chorizo, is sourced from the best suppliers. We offer both freshly butchered and frozen options. Our beef range, known for its traditional cuts and innovative selections, is highly sought after by loyal customers. Additionally, we have reliable partnerships with farmers in Spain and South Africa to ensure a consistent supply of premium turkey, available year-round. Barcino Meats also sources chickens from Spain and Chile, supporting the growth of our suppliers while delivering exceptional products to our customers.
